✦ The Stops

Mon Jul 27 · Night 1 · Sturbridge, MA

Publick House Historic Inn

A 1771 colonial inn on Main Street, with its own tavern so the first night winds down easy. Old Sturbridge Village sits next door for a slow morning before the drive to Hartford.

Grab a tavern table for arrival night — candlelit New England is the right way to open a birthday week.
Confirmed ~55 min from Logan

Wed–Thu Jul 29–30 · Nights 3–4 · Boston, MA

Harborside Inn

An 1846 brick boutique a single block off the water, with Faneuil Hall, the North End and Quincy Market all on foot. Two unhurried nights to wander, eat well, and raise a glass in the city.

The Blue Line's Aquarium stop is right at the door — the whole waterfront is yours, no car needed.
Confirmed block off the harbor
Thu Jul 30 · The Celebration Dinner · Boston

Rowes Wharf Sea Grille

The big dinner of the trip — harbor-view seafood at the Boston Harbor Hotel, oysters to start and the good bottle, a short walk down the waterfront from the Harborside.

Reserved for two — just show up hungry at eight.
